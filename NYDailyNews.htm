New York Daily News Archive

After writing for a number of years at the New York Post, Michael Giltz jumped at the opportunity to write features and Sunday entertainment cover stories at New York’s other major tabloid paper. His features ranged widely from music profiles of artists from every possible genre to features on Tony-winning Broadway shows like The Coast Of Utopia and Spring Awakening to interviews with the stars of Hollywood blockbusters like the men of Ocean’s Thirteen. Michael Giltz has also written book reviews for the Daily News while covering TV, children’s theater, DVD news stories and major events like the Cannes Film Festival.
