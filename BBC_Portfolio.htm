Thanks for taking the time to check out this portfolio I've arranged for you. I've chosen a wide-ranging series of clips. You'll see I've interviewed top celebrities one-on-one, done cover stories and Sunday features, offered political opinion pieces, media criticism and countless reviews. My interests range from all areas of entertainment -- movies, music, books, theater, TV, and DVD -- to sports, politics and religion. I've written extensively for the New York Daily News, The Advocate, New York Post and Entertainment Weekly, as well as the websites Huffington Post and Americablog. I cover the Cannes Film Festival every year, visit London regularly and have traveled throughout North America on assignment. Other publications I've written for include Premiere Magazine, USA Today, the Denver Post, Out Magazine, the Minneapolis Star-Tribune, The Weekly Standard, Disney's Adventures and others. As an expert on pop culture, politics, sports and gay issues, I've appeared on MSNBC and other cable channels, nationally syndicated radio programs, Sirius satellite radio, NPR in Boston, and French, German and Basque television. This year I'm commuting back and forth between New York City and Chicago, so I can report on everything from the heartland to the Deep South (where much of my family lives) to, of course, Manhattan. I look forward to speaking with you soon.
