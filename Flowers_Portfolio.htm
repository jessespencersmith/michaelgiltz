Here's a small sampling of the stories I've written. First, I've included some general pop culture clips. That's followed by a string of stories for "Show Circuit," beginning with a profile of actor John Hensley from the TV series Nip/Tuck. This magazine devoted to horses was on a different topic but similar in some ways to "Flower Magazine." It too was beautifully designed and catered to people passionate about its subject. I covered celebrities who loved the world of horsing and discovered movies, TV shows, books, painters and scultptors related to horsing, as well as profiling experts in the field. I kept my eyes open for upcoming cultural events that could be linked to horsing, from major films (like your "Australia" piece) to Broadway shows and promising first novels. Perhaps I could play a similar role for "Flower Magazine?" I look forward to speaking with you.
