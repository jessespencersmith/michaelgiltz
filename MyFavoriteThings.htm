My Favorite Things The Lists - Michael Giltz

It's one of the burdens of being in the entertainment biz. What's your favorite movie? What's your favorite book? What's your favorite? album? Band? TV show? Musical? Play? Christmas album? You name it! I don't mind someone asking because I've got a list for that. What have you seen lately? Here are lists of everything I've seen each year, in order! What's your favorite film? I've got hundreds! Here's a list of my favorite film for each and every year since 1924! And then I've got lists of ALL my favorite films from last year, 1987, the year you were born, 1939 and so on! I've got lists of books I've read, alphabetically by the author. Favorite TV shows? There's a (woefully incomplete) list! Favorite albums? I've got a list highlighting one album for every year and then dozens for each year and then tons of albums under every artist, rated and ranked and ready to argue about! Enjoy.
