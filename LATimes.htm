Los Angeles Times Archive

Michael Giltz's contributions to the Los Angeles Times came primarily through that newspaper's very popular blog The Hero Complex, a section devoted to all things "fan boy" -- super heroes, sci-fi, graphic novels and the like. Michael's first article for them was an in-depth look at the landmark TV series Twin Peaks that ran both online and in print. However, a number of stories were intended to be web-only and are tagged "online" to differentiate them from those stories that also appear in print.
