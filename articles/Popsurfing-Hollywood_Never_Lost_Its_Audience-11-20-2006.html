The one movie that grossed significantly more is"Finding Nemo," which grossed $339 million and is their biggest hit ofall time.</p><p> Despite what was seen as a "soft" opening, it hit theremarkable sweet spot that Pixar has hit for years.</p><p> And EXPECTINGevery Pixar release to hit $250 million (whatever the budget) is absurd."Cars" grossed less overseas than their last few, hitting "only" $458million worldwide, more than "Toy Story" and "A Bug's Life," about thesame as "Toy Story 2" (at $485 million) and less than the $525 millionfor "Monsters Inc.," the $631 million for "The Incredibles" and therecord-setting $866 million for "Finding Nemo."
