(Much of his woesinvolve trying to file stories via phone lines.</p><p> The only differencebetween Ebert and me is that he eventually figured the French phonesystem out.) His diary is a casual mix of drinking with other reporters,interviewing stars, nodding off during movies due to jet lag and theconstant question of what might win the Palm d'Or.</p><p> Ebert spends a lotof time with low-level personalities and high profile honchos at CannonFilms, mixing in movie set visits for films playing at the festival like"Barfly."
