bad idea that softens the real impact of the play, which is to let our imagination fill in what might have happened and watch adults descend into brutality.</p><p> This is NOT about the boys and their of a play captured on film.</p><p> That apartment (the story essentially takes place over 80 minutes in one location)doesn't feel as claustrophobic as it might.</p><p> Still, there's a certain
