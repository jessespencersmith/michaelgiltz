Any weird gaps (I've read a lot more Dickens than I've listed, for example) are usually because I don't remember with enough specificity what I thought of certain books to rate them.</p><p> Art Garfunkel and Peter Bogdanovich had the right idea! Keep a list, everyone; you'll be glad you did.</p><p> What I wouldn't give for index cards with my brief thoughts on the movies I watched, books I read, albums I heard from a young age
