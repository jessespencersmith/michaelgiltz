Hendryx: It's great and...frustrating at the same time. [said fondly] It's like family getting together again which can be absolutely horrendous at moments and at other times a love fest.HuffPo: Did you really all have a sleepover at Patti's house? Hendryx: We ended up all three of us in the bathroom putting on makeup and getting ready to go out somewhere.HuffPo: If it were a movie, you would have all broken out into song.</p><p> Hendryx: We were! We were singing the parts of "Superlover" because we were going to do it that morning.</p><p> Pat of course always puts on her makeup and then she puts on the same makeup that you've put on, on her, again.</p><p> So she has on makeup for three.</p><p> Like everything it has its challenges.We are individuals and we have our individual issues we deal with.</p><p> But like a family, you rise aboveit.</p><p> We've grown and we are stronger.</p><p> HuffPo : How is Sarah stronger? Hendryx: She's much more confident in her place within Labelle.</p><p> I was the writer, Patti was the lead singer and Sarah was the pretty one.</p><p> Today, she's much more confident that she's integral to the group.</p><p> Without one of those voices, it's not Labelle.</p><p> And Sarah's very acute.</p><p> She'll hearsomething and bring it to our attention that isn't working.</p><p> We all listen differently and she listensin a different way that's very helpful.</p><p> She's very into making sure that we are making the Labellesound.</p><p> She's pinpointed what that is vocally.</p><p> HuffPo: How is Patti stronger? Hendryx: Patti is stronger in her choices.</p><p> Her choices in the past were -- to me -- a lot of the time driven by a need to please other people.</p><p> Yes, she loves and cares about people but she's much stronger about what she wants to do and the music she chooses.</p><p> Even if she likes somebody andthey have a song, she doesn't have to do it.</p><p> She's much more focused in the studio.</p><p> That's not herfavorite place to be; she just wants to sing and get out.</p><p> She's much more present there now andshe'll take the time.</p><p> HuffPo: And how are you stronger?
