Pay teachers -- all teachers -- a lot more and then expect more from them.</p><p> And raising the salary means all teachers, not the sliver of new teachers who enter the profession.</p><p> Half of all teachers will retire in the next decade so the demand for teachers and the need for great ones has never been higher.</p><p> American Teacher is not particularly accomplished or artful, but it is essential viewing for anyone who cares about our education system.</p><p> You'll watch it and want to call your favorite teacher from grade school and thank them all over again.</p><p> On The Bowery
