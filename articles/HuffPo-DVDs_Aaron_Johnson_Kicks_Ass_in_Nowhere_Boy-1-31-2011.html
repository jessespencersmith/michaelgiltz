Twoorphaned siblings are at the heart of the story: a sister who strips at a Tokyo bar and a brother who is dealing drugs.</p><p> At the very beginning of the film, overly aggressive police trying to bust him shoot the brother in the chest.</p><p> We soon get loads of flashbacks showing how he became a dealer, seeing theirparents die in a car crash, watching the brother sleep with a friend's mom Huh?
