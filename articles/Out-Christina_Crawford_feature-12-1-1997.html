Camp Classic: Christina, Darling MOMMIE DEAREST author Christina Crawford (right) proves she's come to terms with her painful childhood and the over-the-top film adaptation of her tell-all by hosting "Christmas With Christina Crawford" at the Castro Theatre in San Francisco on Dec. 19.</p><p> The event promis­ es a parade ofJoan Crawford look-alike s, but don't expect Christina, who now owns a farm in Idaho, to be among them.</p><p> Crawford, age 58, doesn't really understand the appeal of dressing up as her shoulder-padded mom. "But then, I don't like Halloween either." Nor will you find her in the audience during the screening ofFaye Dunaway 's scenery-chewing masterpiece. "Nei­ ther she nor I care to say too much about it." Not to mention wire hangers.
