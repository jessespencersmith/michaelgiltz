Half? A third? A tenth? After the speech, we're toldthe truth.</p><p> According to Sen.</p><p> Joe Biden, only 2,500are fullytrained -- less than two percent.</p><p> How many can fight withour help? About 8,900.</p><p> In total, only about 8 percent can fightin any way.</p><p> Of that 160,000 number he threw out, almost150,000 are not trained in any meaningful way.</p><p> Bushrefuses to say what's going wrong, how he'll speed up thetraining or explain why he is refusing the offers of Germanyand Italy and others to train soldiers in their own countries.</p><p> Butlet's say he doubles the rate of training to 2,500 a year.</p><p> Heck,let's say he quadruples the rate of training to 5,000 a year.How long will it take for Bush to actually reach the level of160,000 troops he implies we have today?
