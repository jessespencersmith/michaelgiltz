Did you think it was an accident when "Idol"finalists going to Hollywood included such a diverse cross-section ofAmerica? How about sitcoms where the best friend/nextdoor neighbor is of a funny/cute/unexpected ethnicity?The simple and not so surprising truth is that EVERY SHOW ON TV ISCAST BY RACE, whether they admit it or not.
