Older PostTHURSDAY, OCTOBER 14, 2021 IRA How To Do a Top 100 List for Decade Genre All-Time Etc When doing a Top 100 Movies of All Time or Top Comedies or Top Silent Films or Top Shorts or Top Films of the 1930s: 1.</p><p> Everyone submits an unranked list of 100 films. 2.</p><p> Those movies are thrown into a big pile with each vote for each film garnering one point.</p><p> We get an overall list of movies, break ties and then vote to eliminate the bottom few so we get a solid 100 films. 3.</p><p> Those movies are presented alphabetically to the group as the 100 movies that will be on the final list. 4.</p><p> Members then rank those 100 films from 100 points at the top for their favorite to 99 points for their second favorite down to 1 point for their least favorite. 5.</p><p> Those points are tallied up and we end up with a ranked list of movies. 6.</p><p> That list is then voted on by batches of ten, starting with the movies ranked at 91-100.</p><p> Members rank those movies to determine their order.</p><p> Then we do the same with 81-90 until the grand finale when we rank the 1-10 films on the list.</p><p> Voila!
