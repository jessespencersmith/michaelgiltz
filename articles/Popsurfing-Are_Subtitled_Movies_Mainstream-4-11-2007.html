"Crouching Tiger Hidden Dragon" was as great aspectacle as anything Hollywood delivered and horror flicks like "TheEye" traveled well too.</p><p> But surely the biggest reason movies have gonemainstream is the multiplex.</p><p> Art films -- and movies with subtitles --aren't relegted to art houses that most people literally never attend.</p><p> Artfilms, whether talky flicks like "Cache" or action films like 'Curse of theGolden Flower" are at your local multiplex, right along "Borat" and"The 300."
