But oops, we're 25 years into the century so maybe it's not too soon to take a first look at all the great albums already out.</p><p> I haven't counted them up, but my list is substantially different from RS.</p><p> I've got more jazz, more world music, certainly more cast albums (Not even Hamilton? Really, Rolling Stone!) and let's face it, more NPR-type music for adults.</p><p> Are they right? Am I wrong? Nope.</p><p> It's just a reflection of how much terrific music is released (literally, thousands of albums worth every week and I'm not even counting A.I.-generated crap!) Plus, we've all got our biases.</p><p> Rolling Stone needs to be hip and reflect the radio, the kids, the cool stuff.</p><p> Not me.</p><p> I'm so old fashioned I listen to albums –not playlists and certainly not random singles.</p><p> So enjoy this list for what it is, a grab-bag of music from the past 25 years I've loved, some of which I've been playing and enjoying for longer than you've been alive (if perhaps we're talking about one of the older albums and you're, say, 21.) Unlike Rolling Stone, I am allowing only one album per act so I can offer up as many different artists as possible. (But I cheat a little.) So enjoy! And let me know what albums are missing from this list!
