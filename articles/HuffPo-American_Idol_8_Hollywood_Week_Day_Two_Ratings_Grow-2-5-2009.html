It's poised to be the #1 show on TV for the fifth straight year and only one other show in TV history did that: All In The Family from 1972-1976.</p><p> No other show has been #1 for five years in any way. (Scroll halfway down this Wikipedia entry to see the list of #1 shows by year.) By that standard, American idol is the biggest TV hit in history.</p><p> Other records are far away: Gunsmoke ran for 20 years, 60 Minutes was in the Top 10 for 23 straight seasons.</p><p> But still, there's no getting away from the fact that Idol is a behemoth.</p><p> And a show that big always has controversies.</p><p> Star Magazine reports that ringer Joanna Pacitti has way too many ties to Idol producers and a track record that would give anyone pause.</p><p> When people complain about contestants who scored a record deal but went nowhere, for example, I always counter: have you ever heard of them?
