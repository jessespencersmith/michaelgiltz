On stage, the actors actually sing the song, though I think it would have been funnier to have them lip sync, just like in the film.</p><p> It's still the highlight of the stage show, just like in the movie.</p><p> And there'sthe problem.</p><p> All the fun bits of Beetlejuice (just like most of the fun bits in Tootsie ) feel like call backs to what was done better and first onMICHAEL GILTZ AT WORK
