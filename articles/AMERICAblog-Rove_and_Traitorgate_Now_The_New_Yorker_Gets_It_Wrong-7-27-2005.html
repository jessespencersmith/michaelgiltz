(I won't even get into the more subtly derogatory descriptionof Wilson as "flamboyant," ie., someone not to be taken seriously.)Does The New Yorker really believe Joe Wilson committed apostasy?By what possible standard could he be accused of this? Joe Wilson isa brave American, described as "a hero" by Bush Sr.</p><p> He came forwardbecause he knew President Bush had lied to the American people inthe State of the Union address during the buildup to war.</p><p> Bush madea claim that had been investigated and proven to be false -- not onlydidn't Bush know it to be true he had every reason to believe it wasfalse to pretend Hussein was trying to buy uranium in Niger.Wilson came forward even though he knew he would be attacked andsmeared just like everyone else from the Administration who cameforward and spoke the truth.</p><p> And Wilson was smeared and attackedand belittled.</p><p> His wife was smeared and attacked and belittled.</p><p> Hischildren were put in harm's way.</p><p> National security was weakened andpeople around the world who risked their lives by working with theUS to spread democracy and freedom might very well be dead.How did this all happen? It happened because Karl Rove, ScooterLibby and perhaps at least one other White House figure outed acovert CIA agent -- the first time in history that an agent has beenouted by political operatives -- just to provide payback againstWilson.</p><p> If anyone should be accused of being an apostate, it's Roveand Scooter and Bush.Keep in mind: Wilson was immediately vindicated.</p><p> He came forwardbecause he knew Bush had misled the American people in the Stateof the Union and made fear-inducing claims that he couldn't back up.Wilson published an op-ed and went on TV with his statement.</p><p> Thevery next day, Bush admitted Wilson was right -- the White Housecalled major media outlets and made clear it wasn't standing bythose 16 words in the SOTU that Wilson knew were wrong.</p><p> Bushcaved immediately.</p><p> EschatonPretty Much - I think this encapsulates nicely ourmodern media environment.1 hour ago HuffPost PoliticsHuckabee's Huck Pac In Turmoil With StaffTurnover, Mounting Debts - Former Arkansas Gov.Mike Huckabee's political action committee, HuckPac, is undergoing a "restructuring," ExecutiveDirector Sarah Huckabee said Wednesday....1 hour ago Ben SmithGoing small - My story tonight: President BarackObama came alive about 50 minutes intoWednesday nightâ€™s news conference â€“ whensomebo2 hours ago FiredoglakeLate Late Night FDL: What Eclipse?
