(There are also dozens of screening rooms andtheaters showing movies of all shapes and sizes throughout thetown.) People always ask me about the topless beach but there isn'ttime.</p><p> The first screening of the day is always a biggie and it takesplace at 8:30 am.</p><p> From then on it's a roundelay of movies and pressconferences and interviews and photo shoots and parties.As cramped as it is, our apartment inspires jealousy with its centrallocation and the blessing of a washer and dryer.</p><p> The new
