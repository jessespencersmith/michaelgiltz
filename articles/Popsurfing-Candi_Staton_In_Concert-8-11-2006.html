(I don't think I've ever heard a music act thankthe press before.) "I'd Rather Be An Old Man's Sweetheart (Than AYoung Man's Fool)" was great, as were her covers of Merle Haggardand Elvis Presley and Tammy Wynette.</p><p> And the gay boys in the crowdloved the finale, which included her big dance hits "Young Hearts RunFree" and "Victim," neither of which is a good showcase for her voice.But the show was entertaining throughout (especially the dorkyArgentinian in front of me who would insist on dancing -- I wisely justleaned against the wall and tapped my toes).</p><p> And her performance ofthe title track of her new album -- the quietly devastating "His Hands"-- was a showstopper.</p><p> I can't remember the last time everyone atBowery Ballroom shut up for a slow number, but this was the sort ofperformance where even the bartenders stopped serving drinks so theycould listen.POSTED BY MICHAEL IN NEW YORK AT 11:30 AM 0 C O M M E N T S :Post a CommentL I N K S T O T H I S P O S TCreate a LinkHomeSubscribe to: Post Comments (Atom)Next Blog»sal1mineo@hotmail.comSEARCH BLOG FLAG BLOGFOLLOW BLOGP O P S U R F I N G .</p><p> C O MS U R F I N G T H E WAV E S O F P O P U L A R C U LT U R EB Y M I C H A E L G I LT Z & F R I E N D SPOPSURFING.COM: Candi Staton In Concerthttp://popsurﬁng.blogspot.com/2006/08/candi-staton-in-concert... 1 of 37/28/09 9:11 PMFAV O R I T E L I N K SAmericablogFive O'Clock Lightning baseball blogDeep Pop -- Lori Lakin's BlogThe Back Page -- Jason Page on ESPNRadioCine-Blog -- George Robinson's BlogDocuments On Art & Cinema -
