Oops.</p><p> There goes that "theearth is 6000 years old" argument.Many weeks, almost every story in the section will includesome scientific fact that makes a mockery of the arguments ofun-intelligent design.</p><p> Quite simply, it's impossible to stay ontop of the latest scientific news without realizing creationism isboth hogwash and undercut by almost every area of scienceyou can name.</p><p> Try and read the science section of your localpaper without being tripped up by "facts" and see how far youget.</p><p> Add New CommentYou are commenting as a Guest.</p><p> You may select one to log into: Logged in as Logout from DISQUSLogged in asusing Facebook Connect (Logout)Type your comment here.</p><p> Ben SmithGoing small -
