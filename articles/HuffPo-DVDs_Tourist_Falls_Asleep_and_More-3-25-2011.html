"Why are people shooting at me?" he might ask, in a low murmur that barely rises above a whisper.</p><p> Jolie spends the entirefilm posing and looking back over her shoulder with movie star aplomb.</p><p> The director's first film was thequiet German drama The Lives Of Others but he is sorely out of his depth here.</p><p> It's the type of film you probably skipped in the theaters but might just rent or buy on DVD, thinking with these stars, how badcan it be?
