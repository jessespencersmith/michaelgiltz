disintegrate over the dual pressures of his work and the bleakMontana landscape that is weighing his more successful wife down.</p><p> John Gload is a prisoner in his jail who mayfinally pay for a lifetime of indifferent, soulless murder.</p><p> Gload usually despises law enforcement but he takes a shineto Val and their late night heart-to-hearts become an uneasy dance as Val makes clear he'll report any confessionsGload might make and Gload tries to wrangle his way into getting Val to do one final act of mercy for this man whohas never shown any mercy in his life.</p><p> Their dance is magnetic, with the reader wondering if Val will be draggeddown to Gload's level or perhaps Gload raised up to a moment of self-awareness and regret or perhaps both at thesame time.</p><p> The crimes
