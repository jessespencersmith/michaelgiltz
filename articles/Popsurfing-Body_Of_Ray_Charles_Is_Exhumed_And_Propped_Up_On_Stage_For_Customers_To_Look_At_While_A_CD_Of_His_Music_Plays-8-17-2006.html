RayCharles already has a catalog as long as your arm -- why add to it withthis grotesque bastardization? And the guys in the Count BasieOrchestra should be ashamed of themselves for playing on it.</p><p> Ten yearsfrom now they probably WILL be onstage playing this music whileRay's body is propped up on a piano bench for the fans to see.</p><p> Andthey'll have no one to blame but themselves.</p><p> Why couldn't Starbucksjust promote some of Ray's tremendous catalog? Why not urge peopleto buy "Modern Sounds In Country & Western?"
