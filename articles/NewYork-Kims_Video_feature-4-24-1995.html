Video Store If your idea of a good video store is one where an employee responds to a request for the movie Blue with "Derek Jarman's or Krzysztof Kieslowski's?," then head to Kim's Video.</p><p> It's a mini-chain: four locations in downtown Manhattan. numerous movie nerds behind the counter. and a stockpile of out-of-print. imported. and just plain odd videos.</p><p> Its reputation for haughtiness, unfortunately, is deserved.</p><p> Honorable mention: Evergreen Video (228 W.</p><p> Houston St.; 691-7362); the New York Public Library's Donnell branch (20 W. 53rd St.; 621-0618).
