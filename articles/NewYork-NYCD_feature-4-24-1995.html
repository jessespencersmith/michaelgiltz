Used-CD Store There's a strip of stores along St.</p><p> Marks Place between Second and Third Avenues that dominates the used-CD market-it includes such stalwarts as Sounds, Smash Compact Discs, Venus Records, and Joe's Compact Discs.</p><p> Even more stores are now setting up shop on the surrounding blocks, so it seems like the last place in the world someone would want to open yet another place.</p><p> Twenty-five-year-old Tony Sachs, a former employee of Smash, was the first to figure that out.</p><p> He has had the Upper West Side virtually to himself since opening NYCD (426 Amsterdam Ave., near 80th St.; 724- 4466) a year and a hair ago.</p><p> Like the downtown stores, his is crammed with used CDs ranging rrom recent releases to obscure jazz classics, as well as a healthy selection of new CDs, boxed sets, and imports.
