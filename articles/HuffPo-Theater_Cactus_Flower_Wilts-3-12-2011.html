Except for one notable exception.</p><p> I'd heard of the show but never saw it or the film adaptation.</p><p> It certainly has a terrific pedigree.</p><p> Cactus Flower was adapted from a French play by Abe Burrows (who should get a much happier revival with How To Succeed In Business Without Really Trying ).</p><p> In it, a dentist tells his ditsy but sweet girlfriend that he's married so she won't get serious.</p><p> When he gets serious, his nurse has to pretend to be his wife to reassure the girl she's no home-wrecker.</p><p> It's no surprise that the nurse loves her dentist and the nebbishywriter next door (Jeremy Bobb) loves the kooky girl.</p><p> Complications ensue.</p><p> Somehow, it was a smash hitand ran for more than 1200 performances.</p><p> Lauren Bacall and Brenda Vaccaro starred, but they were bothshafted when it became a film and their roles were played by Ingrid Bergman (such a great comedianne!)
