Movie Portfolio

This is a portfolio of some of Michael Giltz's many features, cover stores, trend pieces and reviews of movies. Michael Giltz has reviewed movies and DVDs/BluRays extensively for outlets like Entertainment Weekly, the New York Post and Huffington Post. He's done one-on-one interviews with some of the top stars in the world, including Meryl Streep, Nicole Kidman, Julianne Moore, George Clooney, Brad Pitt, Matt Damon, Warren Beatty, and Ryan Gosling, not to mention directors like Pedro Almodovar, Michael Moore, Danny Boyle, Spike Lee, Bo Burnham, David Lynch and Krzysztof Kieslowski. Michael Giltz has been on the set of everything from British comedies at Ealing in the UK to independent films in New Orleans to Hollywood blockbusters on the streets of Manhattan. In his lifetime, he's seen literally thousands of movies and adds hundreds to that list every year.
