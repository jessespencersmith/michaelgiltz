BroadwayDirect Archive

Broadway Direct is a website and newsletter that caters to lovers of theater. Its email newsletter reaches some two million active theater-goers. Michael's contributions focus mainly on books related to theater, such as an annual holiday gift guide, Tony Award tie-ins, monthly picks and regular round-ups of the latest releases.
