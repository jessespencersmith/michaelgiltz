(The color was a cheery yellow.) Now the new CW networkhas chosen a sort of lime green to do the same thing. So what color arethe other networks? CBS Is obviously grey (its viewers are so old), NBCis a sort of faded rainbow (that once glorious peacock is rather mutednow that NBC is in foourth place) and Fox is surely a gaudy neon red(with Fox News sticking to the more basic blood Red). HBO is goldbecause it sets the gold standard for dramas and its audience is sowealthy. Showtime is camoflauge because no matter how good itsshows are the channel remains invisible.
