What We Say Here's a brisk, no-nonsense book on ideas to keep in mind when raising little girls into women. It comes from the acclaimed writer Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie. It began with a request from a friend asking for advice on how to nurture her new childas a feminist. That turned into a Facebook post which spread like wildfire. Now the tips have been gently expanded into a book that can easily be read in half an hour. She offers advice on everything from what colors are appropriate for what gender to whether a woman should take her husband's name...and whether a husband should take his wife's name (becausewhy ask one question if you're not going to ask the other?).
