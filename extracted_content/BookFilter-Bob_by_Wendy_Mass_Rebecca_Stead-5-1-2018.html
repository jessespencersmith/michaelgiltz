What We Say With a well-earned nod to Edward Eager's "Half Magic" and other gentle tales of fantasy, acclaimed authors Wendy Mass and Rebecca Stead team up for this middle-grade story. Eleven year old Livy was just a kid (only five years old!) the last timeshe traveled from the US to visit her grandmother in Australia. So it's no surprise she doesn't remember much of anything about the farm her Gran lives on or the girl Livy befriended at the local restaurant or the green zombie that has been waiting patiently in her guest room closet all this time because Livy said she'd be right back. Wait. What??!!
