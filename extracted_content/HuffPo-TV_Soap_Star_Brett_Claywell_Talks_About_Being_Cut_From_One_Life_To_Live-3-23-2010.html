("Kish" is the nickname for the romance between Kyle Lewis, the openly gay but devious lab tech, and Oliver Fish, thestraight arrow closeted cop). Interestingly, the out gay character is played by Claywell, who's straight, while the closeted cop is played by out actor Scott Evans. In fact, Claywell had just given a previously-scheduled interview to Queer Sighted about his career moments before he walked into the One Life To Live offices -- the actor had intended to turn in his Emmy reel selection, but the his visit soon took an alternate route. "Frank [Valentini, the executive producer] saw me, came and got me, we walked into his office, and he closed both doors," remembers Claywell.
