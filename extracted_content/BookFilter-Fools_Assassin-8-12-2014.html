What We Say In the beginning of a new fantasy trilogy, writer Robin Hobb returns to her most popular characters: Fitz and the Fool. Quite triumphantly so, I'd say. You needn't read everything she's done before to enjoy this tale, thoughclearly fans of the Farseer and Tawny Man trilogies will be the first to line up. Here we find Fitz, a once andfuture assassin, living a quiet life.
