T U E S D AY, A P R I L 2 5 , 2 0 0 6Surfing Through The ObitsI love reading obituaries and today's NYTimes has two good ones. Thefirst is about William Gottlieb, the jazz photographer who capturedlegends like Billie Holiday and Miles Davis at their peak. Whateverimage springs to mind when you think of them, chances are Gottliebsnapped it. But this is the passage that caught my eye:In 1941, he quit his advertising job to do graduate study ineconomics and teach low-level classes at the University ofMaryland, but continued his column.
