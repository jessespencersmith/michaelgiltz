• Omaha (The MOYie) (Bugeater), debuting May 31 in Los Angeles and then head­ ing for 30 other cities, tells a locipy tale about a young Nebraskan chased by jewel thieves. A running gag is that the hero's attractive teen brother, who's gay, gives his itinerant sibling periodic updates on his life: coming out, moving in with a boyfriend, joining the Marines, etc. Yes, this is progress . -MICHAEL GILTZ
