The Fog of War *** Sony Pictures Classics, $26.96 Filmmaker Errol Morris received a well-deserved Oscar for Best Documentary for this fascinating probing of the mind of Robert McNamara, the secretary of defense under presidents Kennedy and Johnson and self-described war crimes perpetrator. Morris lets McNamara apologize, analyze and forcefully defend his actions during World War II and Vietnam.
