FILMMAK ING HAS ITS OWN CLASS SYSTEM: Studio-creat­ ed blockbusters with huge stars (or at least huge budgets) on the one hand, and more economical (and often more human-scaled) independently financed movies on the other. Since the themes are often the same, our selective look at this fall's releases pairs up one film from each side of the tracks, to illuminate the differences-and the similarities.
