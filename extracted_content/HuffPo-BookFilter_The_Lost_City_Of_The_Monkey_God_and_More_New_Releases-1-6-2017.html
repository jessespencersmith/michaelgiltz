Turns out there is, namely a legendary civilization that rivaled the Mayans but disappeared some 500 years ago. Douglas Preston's true-life tale includes everything from the latest technology to ancient curses to scientific backbiting and a mysterious illness that came out of the jungle and is headed your way.... Discover More Here! Love Fiction? Discover More Here! ***************HAPPY BIRTHDAY J.R.R. TOLKIEN! J.R.R. Tolkien was born on January 3, 1892 and if nothing else he was responsible for my favorite Christmas gift ever. One summer in the 1970s, a neighborhood mom pushed me to read "The Hobbit." I came back two days later for her copy of "The Fellowship Of Ring" and two days after that for "The Two Towers" and two days after THAT for "The Return Of The King." I was never much of a re-reader and so it didn't occur to me to even ask for my own copies as a gift. Nonetheless, my mom casually picked up a boxed set of all four paperbacks (for a grand total of maybe $8 at the time?). Christmas morning my parents were thrilled to present a much-needed ten speed bike. It was about as necessary as socks...and just as exciting to me. But "The Lord Of The Rings?!?!" I hadn't expected that yet it was exactly what I wanted and I couldn't wait to dive in again. When my dad realized I'd already read the books, his jaw dropped. Why the heck would I want a copy of a book I'd already read? I felt bad but it really was the best present I ever got. And I devoured them again that Christmas and every Christmas for the next ten years. Tolkien's grandson Simon has a novel set during WWI due out January 24 and Tolkien's tale of "Beren & Lúthien" comes out in a new edition this May...just in time for my birthday. (Yes, mom, that's a hint.) ***************
