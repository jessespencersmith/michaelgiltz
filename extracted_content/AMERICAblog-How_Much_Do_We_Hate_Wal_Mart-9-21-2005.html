Canada knows how to stand up to this behemoth. Workersin the Quebec city of Jonquiere hoped to be the first NorthAmerican Wal-Mart employees to unionize. When they lookedpoised for success...surprise! Wal-Mart shut the store down,something it rarely does. Coincidence? Please. And now thecourts have ruled against it. So how much do we hateWal-Mart?
