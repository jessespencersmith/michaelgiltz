Kims Video feature

Published: 4/24/1995 | Publication: NewYork

Original PDF: Download PDF

Video Store If your idea of a good video store is one where an employee responds to a request for the movie Blue with "Derek Jarman's or Krzysztof Kieslowski's?," then head to Kim's Video. It's a mini-chain: four locations in downtown Manhattan. numerous movie nerds behind the counter. and a stockpile of out-of-print. imported. and just plain odd videos. Its reputation for haughtiness, unfortunately, is deserved. Honorable mention: Evergreen Video (228 W. Houston St.; 691-7362); the New York Public Library's Donnell branch (20 W. 53rd St.; 621-0618).
