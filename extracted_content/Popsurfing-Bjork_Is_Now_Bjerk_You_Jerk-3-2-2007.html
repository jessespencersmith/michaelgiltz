She's just changed the Englishspelling to Bjerk, apparently, to help people on the pronunciation. It'snot "byork" as in "fork" so much as "byerk" as in "jerk." I noticed thenew spelling in a Billboard item on her new CD. And Wikipedia told me"Bjerk" means "a birch tree" in Icelandic and that it's typical forIcelanders to go by their first name only so she's not being arty andoffered a pronunciation guide to saying her name.
