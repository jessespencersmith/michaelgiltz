It was amonster in the ratings and nowthe album is exploding on thecharts. Billboard just releasedmore facts on it. The album is inthe Top Ten and the soundtrackhas spawned eight tracks on theHot 100. Four make their debutthis week, another ("BreakingFree" by Zac Efron and VanessaAnne Hudgens -- pictured) jumps from 86 to 4, the biggest jump inBillboard history. Eight songs in total hit the charts -- they ranked at#4,#28, #34, #35, #43, #62, #67 and #72.
