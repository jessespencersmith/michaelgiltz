For everyyear. For people who always ask "What are your favorite movies,"here's the answer. Yes, I know you can argue about which year certainmovies belong to (especially films from other countries), but the pointis not to make an historical record but just to list some great moviesyou might want to check out. I start with my top pick from each year asa sort of uber list. No, it's not "the most important movies" period, justthe movie that meant the most to me from each year. Some years thechoice was easy, other times a few battled it out or I didn't feel stronglyenough about one particular film to make a statement.
