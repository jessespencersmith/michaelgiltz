Well, what's your domestic policy?Roberts: Again, I think you should just trust me. I'll do theright thing.America: Uh, well, how will you continue the war on terror? Doyou think our ports and and coastlines and borders are safeenough?Roberts: That's exactly the sort of issue I'll be tackling asPresident, so that's exactly the sort of issue I can't commenton. Just vote for me, can't you?America: What about abortion?Roberts: WOAHHHH!!!!!
