What We Say This gentle ode to all things round begins and ends with the same words: "I love round things." The straightforward, declarative sentences of Joyce Sidman don't invite repeated reading and sound more like an adult than the inquisitive,wonder-full girl depicted. It's the warm and inviting images of Taeeun Yoo that save the day. They offer mushrooms that are almost Wonka-like in their bursting glory, eye-catching fields of sunflowers, a gorgeous full moon and enough less- obvious round items to keep kids pointing, without turning the illustrations into a banal guessing game. Not the best, butpleasant enough for nature fans. -- Michael GiltzShare This Book About The Author Joyce Sidman, Taeeun Yoo MoreThe Newbery Honor winner Joyce Sidman is today's foremost nature poet for children.
