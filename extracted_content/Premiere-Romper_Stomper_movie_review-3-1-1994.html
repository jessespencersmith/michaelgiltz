Romper Stomper movie review

Published: 3/1/1994 | Publication: Premiere

Original PDF: Download PDF

* Romper Stomper. Two good reasons to see this tough. dead-on look at neo-Nazi skinheads in Australia: First, Russell Crowe, very good in Proofand mesmerizing here, whether he's attacking Asians or sweetly reading Mein Kampf to his girlfriend. Second, a grip­ ping, brilliant set piece in which the skinheads gang up on some Asians, only to find themselves rightfully hunted down by the victims' buddies; the action is so relentless that you even em­ pathize with the bastards, if only for a moment. Academy En­ tertainment, $92.95; laserdisc, $39.99. MICHAEL GILTZ MARCH 1994 97
