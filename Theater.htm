Theater Portfolio - Michael Giltz

This is a sampling of the theater work Michael Giltz has written over the years, from features trumpeting critical and popular successes like “Spring Awakening” and “The Coast Of Utopia” to profiles and reviews. Michael Giltz’s first introduction to professional theater was a VERY old Rex Harrison in the umpteenth revival of “My Fair Lady” then touring South Florida in the late 1970s or early 1980s. Since then he’s been an avid theater-goer in both New York and London, where the theater isn’t necessarily better but is most certainly cheaper and easier to see. More recent interviews incorporate video and can't be hosted on this site.

To see my interview with playwright Lucas Hnath about his play "A Doll's House, Part 2," follow this link to my May 2017 Hnath interview.
